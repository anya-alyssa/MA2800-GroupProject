MA2800: Live Reports - Climbing, Tiktok Shop and Pokemon-Go

- Introduction -

This project presents a series of live reports touching on three different experiences, each report capturing events as they happen in real time. Our projects focus on participation, community, and digital interaction across three very different events, including a climbing competition, shopping on TikTok live, and a Pokémon-GO community event.
